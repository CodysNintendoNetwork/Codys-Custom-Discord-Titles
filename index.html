Cody's Custom Discord Title Request

Welcome to Cody's Custom Discord Title Request page! With this userstyle, you can add a custom title by your name in Discord. Simply fill out the form below and submit your request to get your account added to the style.

Installation Steps:

1. Install the Stylus extension for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Opera).
2. Click the following link to install the custom title userstyle: Install Cody's Custom Discord Title Userstyle.
3. That's it! You're done. You can now enjoy your custom Discord title. You can check for updates or manage the userstyle from within the Stylus extension.
